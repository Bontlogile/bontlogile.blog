This kind of love, shown without expectation, reflects the very love described in John 3:16. In this verse, God's love isn't distant or conditional. It's a powerful, sacrificial love that moves Him to give His only Son, Jesus, for people He had never met before, and whom He invites to receive His gift of eternal life. Just like the stranger who helps us when we least expect it, God's love reaches out to every person in the world, no matter who they are or where they come from. It's a free gift.
