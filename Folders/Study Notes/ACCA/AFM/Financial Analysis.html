Financial analysis

Introduction

It not about calculating every ratio imaginable; its about calculating a ratio that would help the user get to an informed decision.

The user could want the ratio anlalysis to make an informed decision based on the profitability, liquidity, gearing and investment returns of an organisation.

For Example:

  • Profitability
  • Liquidity
  • Gearing
  • Returns on investment

Take note that most measures will not mean much on their own, and can only be useful when compared with something. This comparison could be against previous years of the same company, with those of similar company or against industry averages.
